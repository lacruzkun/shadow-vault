## Introduction
This section is dedicated to reviewing how programming language interpreters are implemented and executed. To build an interpreter that is light weight, beginner friendly and has a rich type system even though it's an interpreter, we have to look at how existing interpreters have handled or struggled with this balance, because i believe types are an important aspect of programming. Because of the nature of computing, implementation of interpreters leads to technical tradeoffs, with developers choosing specific tradeoffs for thier particular circumstance. I will be looking into different interpreter across different paradigms and looking at the problems faced and solved.
